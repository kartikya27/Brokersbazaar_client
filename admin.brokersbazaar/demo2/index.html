Mission

Mission of ABSC is to bring a significant approach in the field of Business Consulting. Our goal is to ‘Sign a ‘TRUST’ and not a ‘CONTRACT’ with our clients. Pillars of Trust, Transparency, Innovation and Scalability are strength of ABSC.
To make knowledge transfer an affordable element for passionate and serious learners.
To enable businesses shift from reactive to proactive and predictive path.

Vision

ABSC is committed to change the perception of Business Consulting to Business Partnership. From ‘Identification to Implementation’ Business Consulting in the field of banking, education, telecom and Supply Chain are primary focus.
Our vision includes making business consulting sustainable and affordable for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises also.
Vision to design affordable Training Programs in the field of Business Analytics & Data Science for professionals with low and no budget for upskilling.
